Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a ckassic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar (about 24 oz) spaghetti sauce
  • 1 package (12 oz) lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions; drain.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef and drain excess fat. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up.
  7. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 10–15 minutes until cheese is golden.
  9. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
